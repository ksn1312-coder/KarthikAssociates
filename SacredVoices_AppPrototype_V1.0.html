About
Facts
Prayer Times

The Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the most famous Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh — one of the oldest living cities in the world — the temple stands on the western bank of the holy Ganga.

The main deity, Lord Vishwanath (Shiva), is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the holiest of Shiva shrines. The temple has been destroyed and reconstructed several times throughout history.
