When Hourly Billing Makes Sense

✅
Best For Hourly
Ad-hoc advisory · Short-term projects (2–8 weeks) · Due diligence & reviews · Board / pitch prep · Under 5 hrs/week
🔄
Consider Retainer Instead
Ongoing 15+ hrs/month · Need continuity · Predictable costs · Team leadership · Strategy + execution over 3+ months
Retainers offer 10–15% discount over equivalent hourly, with guaranteed availability and deeper immersion.
