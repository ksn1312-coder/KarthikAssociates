02 / The Problem

Millions visit.
None are heard.

A language chasm between India's faith heritage and the people who seek it

The Language Gap
India has 22 scheduled languages and 5 lakh+ faith sites — yet virtually no site offers multilingual audio guides. A Tamil Catholic visiting Velankanni, a Punjabi Muslim at Ajmer Sharif, a Bengali at a Kolkata church — none receive audio in their own language.
The Experience Deficit
Existing guides are either English-only or require expensive human guides. No scalable multilingual solution exists for India's faith sites — temples, mosques, churches, gurudwaras, Buddhist viharas, Jain sites across all traditions.
The Missed Opportunity
65% of Indian internet users prefer native-language content. India's audio market grows at 37% CAGR. WhatsApp audio & devotional streams grew 300%+ post-COVID. Yet no player has built a faith-specific multilingual audio layer.
"The richest stories in India — carved into ancient stone, whispered by priests, carried across millennia — remain inaccessible to most visitors because they arrive in the wrong language."

09 / Technology Architecture

Built for India's scale.

GCP-native · offline-first · DPDP compliant · scholar-verified pipeline

Frontend + PWA
React 18 + Vite + Tailwind · Workbox Service Worker · HLS.js audio · IndexedDB offline · Google OAuth. QR → browser → audio in <3 seconds. No app store needed.
AI Content Pipeline
LangChain + ChromaDB RAG · Claude 3 Sonnet (primary) + Gemini 1.5 (batch) · fact-verification loop · scholar review gate. Confidence score >0.85 auto-publishes.
Translation Layer
Bhashini API (GoI, free) · IndicTrans2 (BLEU 40.2, AI4Bharat) · GPT-4o for Arabic/Pali/Syriac. Marginal cost: ~₹4 per 1,000 audio minutes.
Voice Synthesis
Sarvam AI (11 Indian languages, primary) · ElevenLabs (premium foreign) · Google WaveNet. MOS >3.8 quality gate · SSML-aware narration style per faith tradition.
Infrastructure
GCP India · Cloud Run (auto-scale 0→N) · Vertex AI T4 GPU · Firestore + Cloud Storage + Cloudflare CDN · BigQuery analytics. Est. ₹6,900/month at 10K users.
